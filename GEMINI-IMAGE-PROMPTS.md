## PROMPT MODIFICATION GUIDE

### Adjusting for Dark vs Light backgrounds:
- **Dark (dark mode):** Use `Background: deep ink (#0E1B1E)` + `Logo: white version of Yedda.ai wordmark`
- **Light (editorial):** Use `Background: near-white (#FAFCFC)` + `Logo: teal version of Yedda.ai wordmark`

### Adjusting tone:
- **More urgent/alert-focused:** Add `Orange (#F07830) alert indicators, pulsing dots, warning badges`
- **More calm/analytical:** Replace orange accents with `teal variants (#3A909A, #2A7A84)`, remove pulsing elements

### For carousel slides (must be consistent series):
Add to every slide prompt: `IMPORTANT: This image is slide [N] of [total] in a carousel series. Visual style, color palette, typography feel, and composition must be identical to the other slides in the series. Same background treatment, same font weight, same logo placement.`

### Logo placement rules:
- **Bottom-right:** default for most images
- **Bottom-center:** for centered/symmetric compositions (G008 stat card)
- **NEVER:** overlay logo on faces, complex backgrounds, or over data visualizations
- **ALWAYS:** include on a flat-color field (white, dark, or teal badge) — never floating on a photo
