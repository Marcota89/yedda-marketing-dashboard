The Challenge
A precision electronics manufacturer in Singapore was experiencing recurring line stoppages that were only identified after production quotas were missed. Root cause analysis took 2–4 days per incident. Management lacked real-time visibility into station-level bottlenecks and operator behavior patterns that preceded failures.
Yedda Module Applied
Analytics — Yedda's analytics module correlated CCTV footage with production line sensor data and shift logs. Predictive alerts triggered 15–20 minutes before stoppage events.
